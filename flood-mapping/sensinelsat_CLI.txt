$ sentinelsat -u <user> -p <password> -d --name S1A_IW_GRDH_1SSV_20150320T114745_20150320T114810_005115_0066FA_82D7

$ sentinelsat -u <user> -p <password> -d --uuid c37f3458-7375-4db8-aeb9-0f48563a2a9b

Query images for info:
sentinelsat -u <user> -p <password>  -g some-polygon.geojson -s 20160213 -e 20160216 --producttype GRD
